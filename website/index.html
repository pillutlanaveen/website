Part I - Naruto

Naruto (ナルト) is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Masashi Kishimoto. It tells the story of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja who seeks to gain recognition from his peers and also dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village.

Part II - Naruto Shippuden

“Naruto: Shippuden” is adapted from Part II of the “Naruto” comic book series by Masashi Kishimoto. It is set two and a half years after Part I in the Naruto universe, following the ninja teenager Naruto Uzumaki and his allies.

Boruto-Naruto Next Generations

Boruto is a spin-off and a sequel to Masashi Kishimoto’s Naruto, which follows the exploits of Naruto Uzumaki’s Son, Boruto Uzumaki, and his ninja team. Boruto originated from Shueisha’s proposal to Kishimoto on making a sequel to Naruto.
